## Who we are

Fair Bills UK is a fictional campaign used as placeholder content inside this template. For a live site, replace this section with your organisation name, legal entity, postal address, and the best contact route for privacy queries.

## What data a campaign site may collect

A real campaign site may process personal data when people:

- contact the organisation by email
- use a supporter form or signup journey
- submit stories or case studies
- browse the site and trigger basic analytics

## Why that data might be used

Typical purposes include supporter communications, campaign administration, responding to enquiries, measuring site performance, and maintaining security. A live organisation should explain its lawful bases and any third-party processors clearly.

## Retention and rights

A real privacy notice should say how long personal data is retained, how people can request access or correction, and how to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office if they are unhappy with how data is handled.
